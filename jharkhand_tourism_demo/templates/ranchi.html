About Ranchi

Ranchi, the capital of Jharkhand, is a city blessed with immense natural beauty. Nestled in the Chota Nagpur plateau, it is renowned for its lush green hills, barren rocks, and enchanting waterfalls. The city's pleasant climate and scenic landscapes make it a perfect getaway for nature lovers.

Top Attractions

Hundru Falls

One of the most famous waterfalls in the region, where the Subarnarekha River falls from a height of 322 feet.

Dassam Falls

Also known as Dassam Gagh, this spectacular waterfall is a tributary of the Subarnarekha River.

Tagore Hill

A scenic spot connected to the famous poet Rabindranath Tagore, offering panoramic views of the city.
