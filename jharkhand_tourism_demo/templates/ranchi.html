Ranchi

Ranchi, the capital of Jharkhand, is a city blessed with immense natural beauty. Nestled in the Chota Nagpur plateau, it is renowned for its lush green hills, barren rocks, and enchanting waterfalls, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers.

Hundru Falls

The stunning Subarnarekha River falls from a height of 322 feet.

Dassam Falls

A spectacular waterfall also known as Dassam Gagh.

Tagore Hill

A scenic spot offering panoramic views of the city.
