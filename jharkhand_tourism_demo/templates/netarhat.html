Netarhat

Netarhat is a serene hill station, often called the "Queen of Chotanagpur." Famous for its glorious sunrises and sunsets, the town is covered with dense forests and offers a tranquil retreat from city life with its cool, pleasant climate.

Magnolia Sunset Point

The most famous viewpoint, offering breathtaking sunset views.

Koel View Point

A stunning view of the Koel River meandering through the forests.

Lodh Falls

Jharkhand's highest waterfall, cascading down from 469 feet.
