About Netarhat

Netarhat is a serene and picturesque hill station, often called the "Queen of Chotanagpur." Famous for its glorious sunrises and sunsets, the town is covered with dense forests and offers a tranquil retreat from city life with its cool, pleasant climate throughout the year.

Top Attractions in Netarhat

Magnolia Sunset Point

The most famous viewpoint in Netarhat, offering breathtaking views of the sun setting over the lush hills and valleys.

Koel View Point

A beautiful spot that offers a stunning view of the Koel River meandering through the dense green forests below.

Lodh Falls

Located a short drive from Netarhat, this is Jharkhand's highest waterfall, cascading down from a height of 469 feet.
