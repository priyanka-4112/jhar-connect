Betla National Park

Betla National Park is a beautiful and vast park that was one of the first in India to be declared a tiger reserve. It is home to a rich diversity of wildlife, including elephants, leopards, and various species of deer within a stunning landscape of lush forests and grasslands.

Jeep Safari

Embark on a thrilling safari to spot wildlife in their natural habitat.

Palamu Forts

Explore the historic ruins of two large Chero king forts inside the forest.

Elephant Rides

Experience a unique perspective of the forest with a guided elephant ride.
